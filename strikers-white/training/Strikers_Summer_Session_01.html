First Session Together
Strikers White + Blue  |  U13  |  2 Hours
~16–17 Players Summer Mode

First session post-tryouts. Half the room is new. The goal tonight is getting to know each other through the game. Let it be loud, let it be chaotic, let them figure things out. Read the room. Learn names.

01 Around the World 20 min
Setup
  • 3 fields side by side, 23x30 each
  • Small goals or pugg goals on each field
  • 3 fields in play simultaneously
  • Pink pinnies vs blue pinnies
  • No GKs
The Rule
  • Score a goal and rotate to next field immediately
  • Blue rotates: Field 1 → 2 → 3 → 1
  • Pink rotates: Field 1 → 3 → 2 → 1
  • Teams will be overloaded and underloaded
  • No score kept
Hidden objective: Players who score on all three fields have gone Around the World. They will figure it out on their own mid-game.
02 Dynamic Warm-Up + Passing Series RUN IT 10 min
All players together  •  Coaches lead
Mirrored Dynamic Warm-Up
  • High knees, butt kicks, side shuffles
  • Hip openers, leg swings
  • Lunges with rotation
Passing Series
  • Progress into passing patterns with a ball
  • Keep it moving
03 Rondos / GK Wars 20 min
Field Players — Rondo
  • 4v1 or 5v2 depending on numbers
  • Defenders rotate in on turnover
  • Track passes before turnover
Goalkeepers — GK Wars
  • Two small goals, tight space
  • GKs rotate through as keeper
  • High reps, track saves
Game-time call: 2+ keepers present, split them off for GK Wars. Otherwise everyone stays in rondos.
04 World Cuppies 30 min
Setup
  • 4v4, four teams
  • Mix White + Blue on same teams
  • Teams pick country names
  • Two small pitches side by side
  • No GKs
Format
  • Round robin: 3 games each
  • 3rd/4th place game
  • Final: 1st vs 2nd
  • 4-minute games, 1-minute rotation
Bracket
  • Game 1: A vs B  /  C vs D
  • Game 2: A vs C  /  B vs D
  • Game 3: A vs D  /  B vs C
  • 3rd/4th: 3rd vs 4th
  • Final: 1st vs 2nd
Timing
  • Round robin ~20 min
  • 3rd/4th + Final ~10 min
17 players: Three teams of 4, one team of 5. Rotate the extra player in or let the team self-manage.
05 Flex Scrimmage ~35 min
Format
  • Full group, two even teams
  • Big field, full goals if available
  • GKs in goal
  • No constraints
Your Role
  • Minimal intervention
  • Keep learning names
  • Flex 10 minutes either direction
Close Out
Cool-Down & Team Talk — 5 min
  • Static stretching: quads, hamstrings, hip flexors, calves
  • Quick circle: one word to describe tonight
  • See you on the pitch
