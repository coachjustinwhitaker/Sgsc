Arrival Activation
Complete on arrival before The Foundation. Self-directed. No coaching needed.

Jog the perimeter, then move left to right through the zones. By the time you finish your body is ready to train. This runs every session before anything else.

1
Perimeter Jog  ·  Zone 1
Jog the outside of the course at easy pace before entering Zone 2. Gets blood moving before anything else happens.
2
Lateral Shuffle  ·  Zone 2
Shuffle laterally between the cone pairs, 5 yards each direction. Stay low, push through the hip, do not cross your feet. 8 reps each direction.
3
Forward / Backpedal  ·  Zone 3
Run forward the length of the zone, backpedal to the start. Stay on your toes on the backpedal, keep your eyes up. 4 reps.
4
Touch Grass Walk  ·  Zone 4
Walk forward 10 yards. Front heel down, leg straight, hinge from the hips and sweep both hands just above the ground. Step and repeat on the other side.
5
Agility Ladder  ·  Zone 5
Two feet in each box, quick and light on the toes. Drive the knees, stay tall, keep your arms moving. One run through the ladder.
6
Calf Raises + Ankle Circles  ·  Zone 6
On the edge of the step: 15 calf raises each foot, slow up and controlled down. Then 10 ankle circles each direction each foot.
7
Leg Swings  ·  Zone 7
Hold a goal post or corner flag for balance. Forward and back swings, 10 each leg. Then lateral swings across the body, 10 each leg. Let the leg swing freely.
8
Bodyweight Squats  ·  Zone 8
Feet shoulder width apart, toes slightly out. Sit back and down until thighs are parallel, pause at the bottom, drive through the hips on the way up. 12-15 reps.
